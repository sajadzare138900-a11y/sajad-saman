# sajad-saman
A simple website that provides articles about the transportation system in Iran.
Subway
It's the quickest way to get to where you want.
There are many stations in the city of Tehran. You can pay for the metro using a MetroCard. Another way to pay for it is to buy a single ticket at the station. You can also recharge your MetroCards at a Metro station be The rush hour for the Metro is between 7
and 9 p.m.

Buses 
They are quick but not as quick as the metro. Using them is easier than using Metro.
You can also pay with MetroCards or cash. You can even askdriver to drop you off at the places that are not bus stops.

Snapp
Snapp is a popular online transportation service in Iran. With this application, you can request a car easily on your phone. The price of the trip is shown in the app before you start. Many drivers work with Snapp in different cities in Iran. It is an important part of daily transportation for people.

BRT (Bus Rapid Transit)
Tehran Bus Rapid Transit is the cheapest public transportation service in some cities in Iran and works on special bus lanes. You can wait at a station next to main streets so the buses arrive faster. With BRT you pay a low fare like 7,000 Tomans, which is about $0.16, to travel in the city. Many people use this system because it helps reduce traffic and pollution in big cities.
